Saving the best for last.

Fiat 124 Abarth - 2017

I can't speak first-hand about this car but I can certainly vouch for the Mazda Mx-5 (as it's just a reskinned Mx5 ND) - The fiat 124 was your typical romanticised italian roadster for the people, there were a ton of the classic ones floating about florence when we were there. This modern take (akin to the mustang) allows for historical reminiscing while not having to worry about the lack of headrests, seatbelts, airbags etc of the original model. I can safely say if it were my money i'd go for this or an MX5 in a heartbeat - modern 4cyl sportscars are fun as fuck. Link to Carsales

Seriously though, I've driven the MX5 ND and it's all the sportscar one needs and it has one of the most comfortable dirving positions of any "fun" car i've driven - with the exception of the Jaguar F-Type - that thing was the bee's knees... but also many $$$ more... <_<
